Before everything: LinkedIn access

Reality: I do not have your personal LinkedIn account or a complete LinkedIn database. LinkedIn does not give normal users full access to all company/job/post data, and mass scraping can get accounts restricted.

This tool is built to work with real data in safe ways:

Option 1 — Manual LinkedIn Research

You log into LinkedIn yourself, search jobs/posts/company pages, then paste the evidence here. Safest and easiest way to start.

Option 2 — CSV Import

If you collect leads in Google Sheets, Apollo, Clay, Sales Navigator notes, or any legal data source, export CSV and import it into this tool.

Option 3 — Official API

LinkedIn APIs require an approved LinkedIn developer app and permissions. They usually do not provide complete public search data.

Option 4 — Browser-Assisted Workflow

You can stay logged in on your own browser and use this tool as a research workspace. Do not share passwords. Do not automate mass scraping.

Best plan: Start with manual LinkedIn Jobs + public posts, save 20-50 real companies, then use the scoring system to contact only the strongest opportunities.
