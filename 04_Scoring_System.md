# Automation Opportunity Scoring System

Use this to decide which companies are worth contacting.

Maximum score: **10 points**.

Contact companies with score **7 or higher**.

---

## 1. Repetition Score: 0-2

How often does the task happen?

- 0 = one-time or unclear
- 1 = weekly/monthly
- 2 = daily or many times per week

Examples with 2 points:
- appointment booking
- lead follow-up
- customer support messages
- invoice reminders
- CRM updates
- inventory updates

---

## 2. Cost / Revenue Impact Score: 0-2

Does the problem cost money or block revenue?

- 0 = no clear business impact
- 1 = saves time but impact is small
- 2 = affects sales, customer experience, missed appointments, lost leads, or payroll cost

Examples with 2 points:
- missed calls = lost revenue
- slow lead follow-up = lost deals
- no-shows = lost appointments
- manual invoice follow-up = delayed cash

---

## 3. Simple Automation Fit Score: 0-2

Can we solve it with simple tools?

- 0 = needs complex custom software
- 1 = partly automatable
- 2 = can be automated with forms, Zapier/Make, Airtable, Google Sheets, Notion, email, WhatsApp, CRM, or chatbot

Examples with 2 points:
- form → spreadsheet → email/WhatsApp notification
- website lead → CRM → follow-up email
- appointment form → calendar → reminder
- invoice due date → automatic reminder

---

## 4. SMB Fit Score: 0-1

Is the company a realistic client?

- 0 = too big, too corporate, or unclear budget/contact
- 1 = small or medium business where owner/manager may respond

Best company size to start:
- 2-50 employees
- local business
- founder-led company
- small agency
- clinic/office with clear manager

---

## 5. Decision Maker Clear Score: 0-1

Can you find a person who can say yes?

- 0 = no clear decision maker
- 1 = found owner/founder/operations manager/office manager

Good titles:
- Owner
- Founder
- CEO
- COO
- Operations Manager
- Office Manager
- Practice Manager
- General Manager

---

## 6. Recency Score: 0-2

Is the signal recent?

- 0 = older than 6 months
- 1 = 1-6 months old
- 2 = last 30 days

Recent signals are much stronger because the problem is active now.

---

# Priority Levels

## 8-10 points: 🔥 High Priority
Contact now. Create a custom message and offer a free automation report.

## 6-7 points: ✅ Medium Priority
Good opportunity. Contact if you have time or if the company is in your target industry.

## 4-5 points: ⚠️ Low Priority
Save for later. Need more research.

## 0-3 points: ❌ Ignore
Do not spend time unless new evidence appears.
